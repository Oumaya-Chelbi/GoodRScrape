A Giraffe and a Half,Shel SilversteinA boy and his pet giraffe and considers the question,If you had a giraffe…and he stretched another half…you would have a giraffe and a half. If you dressed him in a suit and he looked very cute... You would have a giraffe and a half with a rat in his hat looking cute in a suit.Delightfully zany rhymes about a giraffe who accumulates some ridiculous things--like glue on his shoe and a bee on his knee--only to lose them again, one by one.
